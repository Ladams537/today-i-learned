Gemini-cli configuration located at: ~/.gemini/settings.json
Can also create a GEMINI.md file which here which will dictate the behaviour of gemini-cli across all of the folders on your computer.

Trade-off between generalisation and optimisation for specific tasks. Utilising protocols is one method for getting around this, and then having increasingly imperative instructions/roles the more locally the GEMINI.md file goes.
